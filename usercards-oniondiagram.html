Container Delivery Co.

User Cards
UC-01 · Executive
CEO
Chief Executive Officer
What they do
Runs the company at the highest level; sets strategic direction and monitors overall performance.
System Needs
  • Real-time KPI dashboard (deliveries, revenue, performance)
  • High-level trend reports without manual requests
  • Company-wide performance visibility
UC-02 · Executive
COO
Chief Operating Officer
What they do
Oversees daily operations across all departments; resolves escalated operational issues.
System Needs
  • View late / stuck jobs in real time
  • Issue flagging and resolution workflow
  • Cross-department operations overview
UC-03 · Executive
CFO
Chief Financial Officer
What they do
Manages all financial matters including billing, accounts receivable, and financial reporting.
System Needs
  • Invoice generation and management
  • Payment tracking and history
  • Unpaid / overdue accounts list
UC-04 · Operations
Logistics Manager
Delivery Operations Lead
What they do
Oversees logistics coordinators and their assigned geographic zones.
System Needs
  • Zone-level delivery status overview
  • Coordinator task completion tracking
  • Issue escalation and resolution tools
UC-05 · Operations
Inventory Manager
Container Fleet Lead
What they do
Oversees inventory coordinators and container management across all zones.
System Needs
  • Zone-level container inventory overview
  • Coordinator performance tracking
  • Issue escalation and resolution tools
UC-06 · Field Ops
Logistics Coordinators
Zone Delivery Coordinator
What they do
Coordinate deliveries within their assigned geographic zone day-to-day.
System Needs
  • Assign drivers to orders
  • Set and manage delivery dates
  • Verify all order information is correct
UC-07 · Field Ops
Inventory Coordinators
Zone Container Coordinator
What they do
Manage the container fleet within their assigned zone, tracking each unit's status.
System Needs
  • Track container location per unit
  • Monitor availability and damage status
  • Track per-container profit data
UC-08 · Front Line
Customer Service
Support Representative
What they do
Handles inbound customer questions, complaints, and order inquiries.
System Needs
  • Look up job status and delivery date
  • Quick customer search (by name, number, etc.)
  • View full order details on demand
UC-09 · Front Line
Sales Agents
Sales & Account Representative
What they do
Acquire new customers, negotiate deals, and manage the order creation pipeline.
System Needs
  • Generate prices and quotes
  • Create new orders in the system
  • Track invoice vs. signed vs. paid status
Onion Diagram
Layer Reference
Layer 1 — Core / System
The central delivery management system itself. All stakeholders interact with this core.
Ring 1 — Executive
CEO · COO · CFO
Highest-level system users; read-heavy, KPI/finance focus.
Ring 2 — Management
Logistics Manager · Inventory Manager
Zone-level oversight; monitor coordinators, handle escalations.
Ring 3 — Coordinators
Logistics Coordinators · Inventory Coordinators
Day-to-day operators; data-entry and assignment heavy.
Ring 4 — Front Line
Customer Service · Sales Agents
Customer-facing roles; search, create orders, support lookups.
External — Customers
End customers; interact indirectly via Sales Agents and Customer Service (no direct system access).
